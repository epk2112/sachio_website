About Us

SACHIO, Safe Hands Allied Community Health Issues Organization, emerged in 2019 as a non-governmental, not-for-profit organization. We are dedicated to building a healthier future for Tanzanian communities through a multi-pronged approach focusing on innovative solutions, bridging gaps between policy and implementation, empowering through knowledge, and building a strong support system.

Services

What We Do.

SACHIO operates under a grassroots model, prioritizing community participation and empowerment. We address broader community health concerns with a particular focus on adolescent health and well-being, encompassing critical issues such as sexual and reproductive health education, mental health awareness and support, nutrition and healthy lifestyle choices, and access to essential healthcare services.

Empowering Choices

We equip young people with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed decisions about their sexual health. This includes education on topics like safe sex practices, contraception, and healthy relationships. We aim to reduce risky behaviors and improve sexual and reproductive health outcomes.

Building Resilience

Mental health concerns are prevalent among adolescents. SACHIO promotes mental well-being and provides a support network for those facing challenges. We work to break down stigma and encourage open communication around mental health, fostering a supportive environment for adolescents.

Fueling Healthy Habits

We educate young people on the importance of healthy eating habits and encourage regular physical activity. This not only benefits their physical health but also their mental well-being and overall sense of well-being. Promoting healthy habits contributes to a healthier lifestyle for adolescents.

Bridging the Gap

Adolescents often face barriers to accessing essential healthcare services. SACHIO works to remove these barriers and ensures young people have the care they need to thrive. We advocate for policy changes and increased resource allocation to improve access to healthcare for all.

Strategic Focus

Our Approach to Adolescent Wellbeing.

SACHIO's strategic plan prioritizes the first three pillars of the National Investment for Adolescents Health and Wellbeing (NAIA_AHW): Preventing HIV, Preventing Teenage Pregnancies, and Preventing Physical, Sexual, and Emotional Violence. We also address other crucial areas through collaboration and by creating an enabling environment.
