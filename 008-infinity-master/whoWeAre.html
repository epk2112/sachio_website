Who we are

SACHIO has been operating in the adolescent health space since 2019, working towards our goal of building a healthier future for Tanzanian communities.

About SACHIO

SACHIO is the only non-governmental organization dedicated to building a healthier future for Tanzanian communities. We strengthen systems and catalyze innovative solutions to make healthcare accessible for adolescents to explore, learn and develop.

Donors and partners
